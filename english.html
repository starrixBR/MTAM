Tour through the 3 metro stations of Samambaia


Terminal Samambaia Station

Discover the Samambaia Terminal Station:

The Terminal Samambaia Station is one of the stations of Distrito Federal metro, situated in Samambaia, followed by the Samambaia Sul Station. Managed by the Companhia do Metropolitano do Distrito Federal, it is one of the terminal stations on the orange line. Here is the location.

Samambaia Sul Station

Discover the Samambaia Sul Station:

The Samambaia Sul Station is one of the stations of the Federal District Metro, located in Samambaia, between the Furnas Station and the Samambaia Terminal Station. Managed by the Federal District Metro Company, it is part of the Orange Line. Here is the location.

Furnas Station

Discover the Furnas Station:

The Furnas Station is one of the stations of the Federal District Metro, located in Samambaia, between the Taguatinga Sul Station and the Samambaia Sul Station. Managed by the Federal District Metro Company, it is part of the Orange Line. Here is the location.
